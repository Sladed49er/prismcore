# Prism Core — Marketing Materials (dynamic folder)

This folder is **dynamic**. It grows as Prism Core is built. Everything that could
become marketing material lands here.

## What goes here

- `WHY.md` — the living narrative / "why" (Simon Sinek framing). The spine of the
  pitch. Updated on every pivot or major feature.
- `screenshots/` — screenshots of the product as it's built, plus anything Matt
  takes and uploads. Each one gets logged in the `WHY.md` screenshot table with date,
  what it shows, and what it can be used for.
- Pitch decks, one-pagers, and conference materials as they're produced.

## How it's maintained

When Matt uploads a screenshot or describes a new feature/pivot, it gets filed here
and `WHY.md` is updated to reflect the current product and reasoning. The goal is
that at any moment this folder is conference-ready.

> Lesson from the Schmitten engagement: great software was built but no screenshots
> or marketing material were captured along the way. Don't repeat that — capture as
> we build.
